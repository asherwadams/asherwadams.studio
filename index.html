Asher Adams

I'm a neuroscience student at Northeastern University in Boston, originally from the Cambridge area. Photography has been a consistent part of my life for the past few years, mostly street and travel work with a focus on daily life and ordinary moments. I'm less interested in the dramatic or confrontational side of street photography and more drawn to light, mood, and the kind of scenes that are easy to walk past.

I've shot across Japan, Hong Kong, China, Belize, Canada, the American West Coast, and the American Northeast, and the portfolio here reflects that range. The work spans cameras I've owned over the years: Sony A7C II, Fujifilm X100VI, and XS-10, each representing a different period of growth.

I'm always happy to connect. For professional inquiries, the contact page is the best place to reach me. For everything more casual, Instagram works better.
